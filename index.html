About me

My name is Alva Löfquist and I am a 20 year old girl currently living in Jönköping! I moved here from Vargön to study at Jönköping University. So far I have enjoyed studying in Jönköping, however I do miss my family. I am the youngest out of of three siblings in the family. There are my older sister Erika, my older brother Simon, my mother Annika and my father Pelle. I really do enjoy spending time with my family and therefore I am grateful that we share a lot of interests. These interests include foremost music and sports, although we do appreciate just spending some quality time having dinner and drinking wine, along with a nice and versatile conversation.
