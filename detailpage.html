Vanja

This is one of those photographs that just happened. I was about to play some theater for children as it was my summer job, when the sky turned gray. My supervisor or theater mentor walked up the grass hill and looked out for signs of a blue sky. I thought it all seemed like a movie poster for a thriller or "Stranger Things" kind of cinematic work. So I took a picture of my theater mentor Vanja.

In my eyes, thiss photograph captures a feeling of melancholy, anxiousness and suspense, but also bravery and curiosity. I imagine it being a photo you see differently depending on your own current mental state.

The picture is taken with an Iphone 8 at a kindergarten in Vänersborg, Sweden.
