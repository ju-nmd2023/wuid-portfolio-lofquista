Organist

This photograph was taken during a quiet night in a small church. Me and two friends had been on a long and cold walk. We had walked and talked for hours in the snowy landscape when our other friend called. With completely stiff and frozen fingers, my friend grabbed her phone and answered that we all would gladly accept the invite to come warm up in the church, where our friend on the other side of the phone was practicing playing the organ. Soon we arrived at the church and everything slowly thawed along with the organ music. Except for the music, everything was quiet and we could enjoy each others company without talking. That is one of my best memories with my best friends.

The picture is taken with an Iphone 8 at a church in Vänersborg, Sweden
